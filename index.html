About Me

My name is Florentin SAROT , an IT Technician born on July 8, 2003, and based in Péruwelz, Belgium. I am driven by a profound passion for new technologies and innovative solutions.

I aim to apply my year of hands-on experience to ensure the optimal functioning of IT systems within a dynamic enterprise.

I am known for my rigor, autonomy, and strong sense of responsibility. My capacity to effectively analyze technical issues and quickly find tailored solutions, alongside my ability to manage stress and remain focused in critical IT situations, are key strengths.

My professional background includes valuable experience as an IT Technician Intern at Cyberdream Leuze (2020-2021), where I gained expertise in workstation installation and configuration, preventive and corrective maintenance, and the implementation of automated data backup systems. I also provided direct technical support to users.

Additionally, I have experience as a Storekeeper Salesperson at Colruyt Group Kraainem (since January 2024) , a Salesperson Intern at Cash Converters Froyennes (2023) , a Production Worker at Cartonnage Lammerant Leuze (2022-2023) , and an Administrative Employee at FJC Metubel La Louvière (2018).

Academically, I obtained my CESS (Certificate of Higher Secondary Education) as an IT Technician from Athénée Royal Péruwelz (2018-2021). I also completed training in Website Promotion and SEO (Technofutur TIC, September-December 2022) and undertook an E-Business formation at Condorcet Tournai (September 2022-June 2023), which was not completed.

I am proficient in English, French, and Dutch, and am mobile by bicycle within the Péruwelz, Tournai, and Beloeil areas for on-site positions. I am also open to remote work opportunities.
